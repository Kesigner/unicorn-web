What is Pool about?

Pool is a platform application that enables musicians to find, connect and collaborate with professionals on a global basis.
Pool‘s goal is to build the „LinkedIn“ for musicians in order to lower the entry barriers into the music industry.

Find your tribe, build your network and fast-track your career in a professional environment.
